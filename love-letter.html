Love Letters



Project Type:
Illustration, Poems

Date:
15 March- 20 May 2022

Location:
Potchefstroom, South Africa



This is my personal artist book that depicts the original five love languages within the context of my own life. The book consists of 10 poems ( two poems for each love language) that are dedicated to various people in my life and an accompanying illustration for each poem. The chosen style was used to imitate the uncomfortableness that usually comes to the surface when you have to claim love for the people in your life ( or at least for me). The emotions I felt when creating this book came out quite difficult due to them feeling sharp and jagged, thus the cubistic style felt appropriate.

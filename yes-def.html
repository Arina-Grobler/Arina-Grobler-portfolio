Yes, Definitely! still water label



Project Type:
Illustration and label making

Date:
22 February - 15 March 2022

Location:
Potchefstroom, South Africa



This was a simulated project that was aimed at creating three labels for Yes, Definitely! The bottles are a chance for artists to get their work out into the world and get some exposure. The labels also didn't have a concept to back them because the brand is concentrated on creating art for the sake of art.

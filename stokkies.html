Stokkiesdraai deur die Namalkwaland



Elements of project
An editorial illustration with an illustrated display font poster

Date
27 July - 5 September 2022

Location
Potchefstroom, South Africa



This project was a simulation where we were tasked to create a typeface poster to accompany an editorial illustration for a magafont of our choosing. I created a typeface that imitates the flowers that grow in the Namaqualand region in South Africa to persuade the readers of Weg! to travel to the area and encounter the flowers in the short period of time that they bloom at the end of winter.

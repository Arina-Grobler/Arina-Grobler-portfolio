Hexlappies branding



Project Type:
Illustration, branding

Date:
11 April - 16 May 2023

Location:
Potchefstroom, South Africa



This project was a simulation constructed at the university where they wanted to introduce us to the concept of working with actual clients. The client was Hexlappies which is an initiative that invited the women of the Hex River Valley to get together and learn new crafting skills that they could use to generate an income for the parts of the year when they don't get income from helping with crops in the surrounding area. We were tasked with creating a brand for them to use when they give feedback to their investors. I worked on this with the lovely Magdeli Venter.

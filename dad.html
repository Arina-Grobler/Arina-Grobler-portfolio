Dead or dim-witted: designed the dad



Project type
Illustration

Date
February - April 2023

Location
Potchefstroom, South Africa



This project was simulated to imitate the process of designing a zine for the Ico-D conference. The Zine was created to create discussion around design ethics under the specific topic of how cartoons designed their dad character to either be dead or dim-witted. This zine aims to persuade designers of the dad character to take the more modern route by creating a well-rounded or more nuanced dad.
